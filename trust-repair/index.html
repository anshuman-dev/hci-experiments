HRI Study — Voice & Trust

Voice & Trust Repair Study

You will interact with a robot navigation assistant across several short sessions. The robot will guide you through a task, make an error, and respond in different ways. You will rate your trust at key moments.

Takes approximately 12–18 minutes. Please use headphones.

No personal data beyond anonymous interaction logs is collected. You may stop at any time. Clicking Begin constitutes consent.

What will happen

  • A robot introduces itself. You give a first impression trust rating.
  • The robot navigates you toward a destination. Watch the progress bar.
  • The robot makes a navigation error — your progress drops. Rate your trust.
  • The robot responds to the error in some way. Rate your trust again.
  • This repeats 6 times across different robots and scenarios.

A short practice trial comes first. Responses during practice are not recorded.
