HRI Study — Voice & Trust

Voice & Trust Repair Study

In this study you will interact with a robot navigation assistant across several short sessions. The robot will guide you through a task, make an error, and respond in different ways. You will rate your trust in the robot at key moments.

The study takes approximately 10–15 minutes. Please use headphones — audio quality matters.

No personal data beyond anonymous interaction logs is collected. You may stop at any time. Clicking Begin constitutes consent.

What will happen

  • A robot will introduce itself. You will give a first impression rating.
  • The robot will guide you through a navigation task successfully.
  • The robot will make a navigation error. You will rate your trust immediately.
  • The robot will respond to the error in some way.
  • You will rate your trust once more.
  • This repeats 6 times. The robot will sound different across trials.

No right or wrong answers. We are interested in your natural reactions.
