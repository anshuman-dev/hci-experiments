HRI Study — Voice & Trust

Voice & Trust Repair Study

In this study you will interact with a robot navigation assistant across several short sessions. The robot will guide you through a task, make an error, and respond in different ways. You will rate your trust in the robot at key moments.

The study takes approximately 10–15 minutes. Please use headphones — audio quality matters for this study.

No personal data beyond anonymous interaction logs is collected. You may stop at any time. Clicking Begin constitutes consent.

What will happen

  • A robot will introduce itself and guide you through a navigation task.
  • At some point, the robot will make a navigation error.
  • You will rate how much you trust the robot immediately after the error.
  • The robot will then respond to its mistake in some way.
  • You will rate your trust again after the robot responds.
  • This repeats across 6 short trials. The robot will sound different in some trials.

There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in your natural reactions.
