Voice & Trust Repair in HRI

A within-subjects browser experiment examining how voice naturalness (synthetic vs. natural, both male to eliminate gender confound) interacts with repair strategy (apology / explanation / silence) to restore trust after a robot navigation error.

Extends Romeo et al. (2025) by adding a pre-registered manipulation check (per-trial naturalness rating), task stakes (animated progress bar that drops on error), and validated trust items from Jian et al. (2000).
